Akilles is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the information you voluntarily provide within the app regarding exercise, and workout information:

The information you input into Akilles is used solely for the purpose of providing you with the app's functionalities. We do not use your data for any other purposes such as marketing, analytics, or sharing with third parties.

Your data is retained for as long as you keep it in the App or in your iCloud account. We do not retain any of your data on our servers.
